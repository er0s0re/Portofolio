Project Overview

Our Data Analytics Dashboard is a powerful tool designed to help businesses make data-driven decisions. Built with Python, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Plotly, this dashboard offers real-time insights into key performance indicators, customer behavior, and market trends.

With its intuitive interface and customizable features, users can easily explore complex datasets, create stunning visualizations, and generate actionable reports to drive business growth.
